1. [github.com](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//github.com/)
2. [assets-cdn.github.com](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//assets-cdn.github.com/)
3. [github.global.ssl.fastly.net](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//github.global.ssl.fastly.net/)
