## Wakatime - see your effort 

<https://wakatime.com/> will automatically log in the time you spend on **IDE, editor, and some websites**. Furthermore, it can recognise which how much time you spend on each programming language, like C++, Python, Haskell. 

Since **Notion** has its webpage client, so you can add <https://www.notion.so/>  into the whitelist. Moreover, please add <https://devdocs.io/> (which is a collection of online documentation browser....C++, Python...). 

Don't forget add <https://codeforces.com/> , <https://leetcode.com/>, <https://vjudge.net/> <https://www.codewars.com/> into wakatime!!!. 



## Notion - the ever best note taking software

### Supporting Web

open <https://www.notion.so/> to start your note taking journey (remember add https://www.notion.so/ to Wakatime chrome extension, so you can log in your time). 

### Organise your notes like Wiki

In notion, you can organise all notes and materials as wiki !

And you can insert Math equation, markdown, code.....

Moreover, you even can embed a HTML file into your note......(which means you can run JavaScript into the notion....)

### Export your notes 

You may worry what if Notion has to close down (hopefully not). 

You can export a page and all its subpages **in HTML format** (without losing any styles in your notes)!
